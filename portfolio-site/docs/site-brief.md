# Site Brief

## Goal
Present Christabel O Frimpong as a builder of AI-powered products, developer tools, and data-informed web experiences.

## Audience
- Recruiters
- Hiring managers
- Technical interviewers
- Potential collaborators

## Content Strategy
- Lead with a sharp personal value proposition.
- Show a curated set of 2026 projects, not every repo equally.
- Emphasize implementation details, product thinking, and maintainability.
- Keep contact paths simple and visible.

## UX Direction
- Editorial studio look with bold typography, layered backgrounds, and asymmetric section rhythm.
- Mobile-first responsive layout.
- Short, scannable copy with strong project positioning.
